New Relic supports the forwarding of logs from AWS services. Our log management solution provides deeper visibility into application and infrastructure performance data, including events, errors, and traces, to reduce mean time to resolution (MTTR) and facilitate quick troubleshooting of production incidents. Discover how you can amplify the power of your AWS logs by forwarding them to New Relic. Gain unparalleled visibility into your system's performance, catch issues before they escalate, and make data-driven decisions with ease. Enhance your team's operational efficiency while maintaining security and compliance.
Want to learn how to get started with New Relic logs management? See [Get started with log management](/docs/logs/get-started/get-started-log-management/).
